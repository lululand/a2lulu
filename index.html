After designing and producing websites and emails for many years, I took the next step and became a web application developer. Tech Elevator in Detroit helped me make the move with a 14-week full-time immersive program where we used Java, Spring, postgreSQL, JavaScript, APIs, and Vue to build web applications. Since then I have been expanding my knowledge, learning and building apps with other tech stacks, most receently using Node.js, Express, EJS, D3.js, and mongoDB.
Things I love: learning, problem-solving, collaborating, research, and design.
